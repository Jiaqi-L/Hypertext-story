A Story of Nan Huaijin

Nan Huaijin went back home with disappointment.
However,a couple days later,he decided to visit the wise man again.
This time,his persistence finally moved the man.


The wise man went out to meet him and asked the reason why he was here.
Nan Huaijin told everything about his struggling, about how life was unfair to him as well as all his misery.
After hearing the complain,the wise man said"I can't tell you exactly how to do to solve all those problems, but I can give you some advice of life."
