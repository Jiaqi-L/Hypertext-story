A Story of Nan Huaijin

Nan Huaijin is a famous contemporary Chinese writer. Legend has it that when he was young, he encountered some difficulties. He was very upset and began to search around everywhere for the answer to life.


At last, after much effort, he found a wise man of high prestige. Nan Huaijin came to the door of this man and knocked.


However,there was no answer.
After knocking for a long time,someone finally came to open the door.
